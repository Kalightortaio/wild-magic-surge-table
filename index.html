Frequently Asked Questions

A surge contains the text "the target of your spell," but the spell was not a single-target spell. What happens?

Multi-Target Spells: The surge affects the first target affected by the spell.

Range of Self Spells: The target is yourself.

Summoning Spells: The target is the creature you summon.

Area-of-Effect (AoE) Spells: The target is the first creature encountered from the spell's point of origin. If no creature exists, the point of origin itself is considered the target.
